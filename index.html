Meet Ashton

Built From the Ground Up.

Ashton Egan picked up his first lawn mower at age 11 and fell in love with the art of landscaping. What started as a passion in his own backyard turned into a craft he could not put down.

In 2024, at 15 years old, he started taking on real clients around East Granby. Now 17, Ashton is scaling Egan Property Maintenance across Hartford County, serving residential and commercial properties throughout the region. Every property gets his personal attention. No crews of strangers, no cut corners.

Just quality work from someone who built this from nothing and takes serious pride in what he puts out.
